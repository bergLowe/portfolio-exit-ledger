# Exit Calculator — Portfolio Exit Ledger

A free calculator for Indian stock market investors that answers one question:

> **"I invested ₹X. I want ₹Y in hand after selling. What price do I need to
> sell at — after brokerage, taxes, and other charges?"**

Just enter what you bought and what you want to walk away with — it works out
the exact price, so you're not guessing or under-selling.

---

## What it does

- Enter your buy lots (quantity + price) — it works out your average buy
  price and total money invested, including all buy-side charges.
- Tell it what you want, in whichever way is easiest for you:
  - An exact **amount in hand**, or
  - A **profit in rupees** you want on top of what you invested, or
  - A **profit percentage** you want on top of what you invested.
- Pick your tax type — short-term or long-term capital gains.
- It gives you the **exact price to sell at**, plus a full breakdown of
  brokerage, transaction charges, GST, taxes, and what you'll actually
  receive.
- All charges are editable if your broker's rates are different.
- Works on any phone or computer, remembers your inputs, and can be added to
  your home screen like an app. Nothing you enter is ever sent anywhere —
  it all stays on your device.

## How to use it

Open the app, add your buy lot(s), enter what you want back, and it shows
you the price to sell at — instantly, as you type.

To install it like an app on your phone: open it in your browser and choose
**"Add to Home Screen"**.

## Disclaimer

This is a planning tool, not tax or investment advice. It uses standard
market rates and current tax rules, but real contract notes can vary
slightly. Always double-check the numbers with your broker and a chartered
accountant before making a decision.
